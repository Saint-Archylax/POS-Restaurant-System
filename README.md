# Integrated Restaurant System for Employees and Owners

A web-based restaurant management system designed to streamline daily operations through order management, point-of-sale (POS), inventory tracking, and business reporting. The system helps restaurant owners and staff improve efficiency, reduce errors, and enhance customer service.

## Features

### Order Management

* Create and manage customer orders
* Support for dine-in, takeout, and delivery transactions
* Real-time order processing

### Point of Sale (POS)

* Fast and accurate sales transactions
* Receipt generation
* Multiple payment methods support

### Inventory Management

* Real-time stock monitoring
* Automatic inventory updates after sales
* Low-stock alerts and inventory tracking

### Menu Management

* Add, update, and remove menu items
* Manage pricing and item availability
* Dynamic menu updates

### Reporting & Analytics

* Sales reports and transaction summaries
* Inventory usage tracking
* Top-selling products analysis

### User Management

* Role-based access control
* Employee and administrator accounts
* Secure authentication and authorization

## Objectives

* Automate restaurant sales and ordering processes
* Reduce transaction errors and service delays
* Monitor inventory in real time
* Generate business reports for informed decision-making
* Improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction

## Key Benefits

* Faster order processing

## Project Scope

The system focuses on:

* Customer order management
* Sales transactions
* Inventory monitoring
* Reporting and analytics

The system does not cover:

* Payroll management
* Employee HR records
* Supplier management

## Business Domain

Developed for a restaurant environment to digitize and optimize daily operations, replacing manual processes with an integrated management solution for both employees and restaurant owners.
